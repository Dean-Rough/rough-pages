Driftwood

A weathered Glasgow pub reimagined. Dark timber, warm leather, and the quiet confidence of a room that's been earning its keep for a century.

The Soul of the Site
The
Brief

"A room you'd choose for two hours, not two pints."

Site Function

Community local with food ambition. Daytime trade through evening — coffee to cocktails, soup to steak pie.

Aesthetic Anchor

Weathered and warm. Nothing shiny, nothing new-looking. Driftwood as metaphor — shaped by time, solid at the core.

Retention Strategy

The chair test. Every seat must feel like the best seat in the house. Leather you sink into. Corners you claim.

Identity
The Brand
Driftwood

Primary. Founders Grotesk X-Condensed. Direct, unadorned, confident. The word itself carries the aesthetic — time-worn, shaped by elements, solid.

Driftwood

Secondary mark. Cormorant Garamond italic. For editorial applications, menus, and moments that need warmth. Pairs the weight of the name with a lighter touch.

Driftwood

Gilded window signage on a dark shopfront. No gimmick, no illustration — just the word, confident and permanent. Black gloss timber, gold leaf lettering, warm internal glow behind frosted glass.

Entry & First Impression

Street door opens into the bar. Line of sight to the back bar and through to The Snug. First three seconds: dark, warm, serious.

Zones

Bar (loud end), The Snug (quiet end), dining nook (mid-room, booth seating). Natural flow from drinking to eating without a hard divide.

Structural Intent

Remove false ceiling in bar to expose original height. Open up snug-breezeway for visual connection. Retain all original cornice.
